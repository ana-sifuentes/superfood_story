The history behind superfoods...

Superfoods are considered better food choices than other other non super ones. While this marketing term can be misleading, the true origin of this foods is often overlooked and forgotten. Let's explore where our food comes from and what impact it has...

The complexity of Food systems

Food systems are complex, there are many players behind the scenes. Goverments make legislation to incentivise farmer to grow certain crops that are then transported, packed by companies and then sold to us in supermarkets. In this simple example, policy, agriculture, manufacturing, marketing and retail are involved. But, do we really know where x is from?

Origin of chia

Chia is actually from mesoamerica, it was used by the Aztecs - an indigenous civilization famous for their strong military, amazing architecture and progressive education.

image showing AZTECS

Lost and Rediscovery

Because of the key role chia had in Aztec society and religion, it was banned during colonization and lost for almost 400 years. It managed to survive in small isolated communities and it returned thanks to terracota decorations, research on its nutritional value and the media discussing it.
